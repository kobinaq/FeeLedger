# UX Principles

FeeLedger is for busy school proprietors, headteachers, accountants, cashiers, and parents. Many users may not be highly technical.

## Main Principle

Every user should understand what to do next without training.

## Admin UX

- Use plain English.
- Show one clear primary action on each page.
- Make important numbers visible.
- Keep reports simple and action-oriented.
- Use guided steps for billing, payments, and payment plans.
- Keep the cashier screen fast: search, confirm balance, enter payment, generate receipt.
- Avoid dense mobile tables where cards communicate better.

## Parent Portal UX

- Show the family balance immediately.
- Show the next payment date clearly.
- Keep navigation short and obvious.
- Use receipt cards and payment plan timelines.
- Do not show charts to parents.
- Make contact and payment instructions easy to find.

## Visual Style

- Primary: deep green `#1B4332`
- Accent: amber `#D97706`
- Background: light neutral grey `#F8FAFC`
- Use calm cards, clear labels, readable text, and restrained charts.
